About Us

At Elliptical Enterprises Limited, we are more than just a business — we are a dynamic force shaping the future. With a diverse portfolio spanning General Trading, Import & Export, Logistics & Supply Chain Management, Real Estate & Property Development, Healthcare Services, and Facilities Management, we are committed to driving excellence, innovation, and impact across every sector we touch. Our name symbolizes progress, adaptability, and forward-thinking solutions tailored to meet the evolving needs of businesses and communities across Uganda and beyond. At the core of everything we do is a simple promises — to inspire transformation and deliver value with integrity.

Vision Statement

To become a leading multi-sector enterprise in East Africa, known for excellence in trade, logistics, real estate, healthcare, and facilities management
transforming lives and industries through innovation, integrity, and inclusive growth.

Mission Statement

To provide high-quality, sustainable, and customer-focused solutions across diverse sectors by leveraging local talent, global partnerships, and cutting-edge technologies — while creating value for stakeholders and empowering communities
