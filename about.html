About Us

Company History and Background

Elliptical Enterprise Limited was established to meet the growing demand for integrated business solutions across multiple sectors in Uganda and the East African region. With its head office strategically located in Wakiso District, the company is positioned to serve both local and international clients across a diverse range of industries.

The company was founded by two visionary entrepreneurs who recognized the need for a dynamic, flexible, and multi-sector enterprise that delivers excellence in trading, logistics, property, healthcare, and facility services. Backed by a combined wealth of experience in administration, operations, and market development, the directors laid the foundation for a business driven by integrity, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Our Pillars

Elliptical Enterprise Limited operates under the following key pillars.

General Trading

  • To engage in the wholesale and retail trade of a diverse range of consumer goods, industrial equipment, and raw materials.
  • To establish partnerships with local and international suppliers to ensure the availability of high-quality products.
  • To provide competitive pricing and exceptional customer service in all trading activities.

Import & Export

  • To facilitate the importation of high-demand products and commodities for the local market.
  • To promote the export of locally sourced agricultural, industrial, and manufactured goods to international markets.
  • To comply with all international trade regulations and build strong relationships with global partners.

Logistics & Supply Chain Management.

  • To provide efficient transportation, warehousing, and distribution services for businesses and individuals.
  • To optimize supply chain operations through technology-driven logistics solutions.
  • To develop strong networks with shipping, freight, and courier service providers for seamless operations.

Real Estate & Property Development

  • To acquire, develop, and manage residential, commercial, and industrial properties.
  • To provide property leasing, sales, and investment consultation services.
  • To engage in real estate development projects that contribute to urban growth and infrastructure expansion.

Healthcare Services

  • To establish and operate healthcare facilities, pharmacies, and medical supply distribution centers.
  • To provide high-quality medical equipment, pharmaceuticals, and wellness products.
  • To partner with healthcare professionals and institutions to enhance medical service delivery.

Facilities Management

  • To provide integrated facilities management solutions, including cleaning, security, and maintenance services.
  • To ensure the upkeep of residential, commercial, and industrial properties through professional management.
  • To implement sustainable and cost-effective facility maintenance strategies.
